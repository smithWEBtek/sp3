Bio

Brad Smith is a veteran keyboardist of funk and Latin music in Boston. He has worked as a show band pianist on cruise ships, accompanying a wide range of vocalists from jazz, classical to R&B and contemporary. His musical loves are funk, and old school Salsa. He has recorded as a sideman on eight albums and has been fortunate to work with many of the great players in Boston and New York.

Brad is available for performing, touring and recording anywhere in the world.
