REBUILDING, REGULATION, TUNING AND VOICING SKILLS

Tuning as many as 1200 pianos per year between 1981 and 2011, my specialty is concert regulation, aural tuning and voicing. Aural tuning is my preference. In 2006 I completed renovations on a 3000 sq ft. piano rebuilding & refinishing shop in Bedford, NH providing complete rebuilding and refinishing services, specializing in Steinway restoration In March of 2007 I replaced hammers/shanks/flanges on the concert Steinway D for St. Paul's School in Concord, NH in preparation for an April 12th concert by artist, Christopher Taylor. Mr. Taylor played a challenging program, including all of the Chopin Etudes. We worked together to voice the piano as evenly as possible, including full shift voicing and half-shift voicing, for maximum dynamic possibilities. As an active rebuilder with PTG, I keep abreast of the latest tools and methods for the myriad tasks involved in rebuilding a piano. I maintain an extensive array of tools, jigs, machines and fixtures devoted to the highest possible quality rebuilding
